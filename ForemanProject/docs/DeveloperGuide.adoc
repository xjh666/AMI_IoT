== Setting up

=== Prerequisites

. *JDK `1.8.0_60`* or later
. *Android Studio* IDE

=== Importing the project into Eclipse

.  Fork this repo, and clone the fork to your computer
.  Open Android Studio 
.  Click `File` > `New` >`Import Project...`
.  Locate the project's directory and select `ForemanProject`
.  Click `OK`
